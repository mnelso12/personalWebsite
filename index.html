Madelyn loves coding

With a degree in Computer Science from Notre Dame, Madelyn is comfortable in several computer languages, preferring JavaScript, Python, and C++. But that's not all - she runs, knits, plays the violin in the Notre Dame Symphony Orchestra, creates pottery, and plays the trumpet in America's first university band: The Band of the Fighting Irish. She is a proud synesthete who never underestimates the importance of color.

she does mobile and web

Madelyn has contributed to eight mobile apps, five of which were individual projects, and four of which are currently available on the App Store. She's also worked on several web apps, developing a web tool for AbbVie's antibody engineering group and receiving recognition for front-end work at collegiate hackathons.

her passion is computer science education

For her senior thesis, Madelyn created a web app for providing resources to Americans of all ages interested in pursuing computer science education. She received a grant to study the rapid increase of computer science education in England, and plans to direclty apply her research to better computer science education in the US. In her free time, she volunteers as a coding instructor at a local elementary school, and teaches coding to local, underserved youth through the South Bend Code School.
